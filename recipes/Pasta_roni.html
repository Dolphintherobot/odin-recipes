Pasta Roni

Description:

Call it a childhood craving, dorm room gourmet,

or simply a grocery store pantry staple, but homemade angel hair pasta with herbs is just as tasty and fast to make!

There's no denying the simplicity

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 ounces angel hair pasta
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • ¾ cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley, or to taste
